French Toast

About French Toast

So basically bread toast but the bread will be dipped in a bowl of egg which will be seasoned. It tastes a little sweet and mainly fills our tummy pretty quick like with just 2 breads.

Ingredients for making 2:-
  • Eggs x 3
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Spicy Powder
  • Oil
  • Ghee
Recipe:-
  1. Turn the stove on with a frying pan on it, let it heat.
  2. Take a bowl and crack all the eggs and mix it with enough salt, pepper and spicy power.(can also add vegies but i hate them)
  3. Once the pan is heated toast the bread with ghee but just a little then dip it in the bowl of egg and toast it again.
  4. To get the sweet taste you can a small amount of sugar to the bowl.
