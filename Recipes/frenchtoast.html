French Toast

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Bread
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Recipe:

  1. Crack the egg and put it in a container.
  2. Add seasonings to it and stir it until it is mixed completely.
  3. Pour the egg on to a pan and start frying it.
  4. After about 15sec put a semi complete toast over the egg.
