Steamed Groundnuts

About Groundnuts

Groundnuts are really healthy as they provide nutritions and the good one's are very tasty its a litle sweet and can be very helpful to just chew on something and also pass time, since we gotta break open the shell and when it's steamed it gets easier to break open.

Ingredients:-
  • Groundnuts atleast 200grams
  • Salt
  • Water
Recipe:-
  1. Clean the grounduts as it might contain soil on it.
  2. After cleaning put them into a pressure cooker.
  3. Fill it with water until the water level is over the groundnuts.
  4. Add 3 teaspoons of salt for 200 grams of groundnut.
  5. With flame not very high wait for 3 whistles and turn the stove off.
  6. Wait 15mins before opening the cooker and enjoy.
